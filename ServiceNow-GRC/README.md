# 🏢 ServiceNow GRC / IRM Notes

Originally, I mostly associated ServiceNow with ticketing & project management.

The deeper I explored the IRM/GRC side, the more I realized how much broader the platform has become in enterprise environments.

What stood out to me most was the relationship between governance, workflows, policy management, entity structures, risk visibility, compliance coordination, & operational standardization across large organizations.

The platform feels less like a traditional ITSM tool & more like enterprise operational infrastructure once you start seeing how the pieces connect.

Another thing I genuinely appreciated was the quality of the learning ecosystem itself. The browser-based training, guided labs, progress tracking, certifications, & learning paths made the platform approachable while still exposing deeper enterprise concepts.

This section documents observations, notes, screenshots, architecture concepts, workflows, & practical takeaways from the ServiceNow IRM Fundamentals course & related exploration.
